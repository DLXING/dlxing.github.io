Group members

Ruyun Zhang, PhD student
Ruyun got her M.Sc degree in ecology from South China Botanical Garden, CAS and joined the lab in Sep. 2020. Her research is focused on the scale-dependent driving mechanisms of spatial beta diversity. Ruyun is also interested in many aspects of intraspecific trait variation.

Xiaoning Wang, PhD student
Xiaoning got her M.Sc degree in ecology from Fujian Normal University and joined the lab in Sep. 2021. Her research is focused on the global patterns and underlying drivers of temporal change in beta diversity.


Wenhao Zhao, PhD student
Wenhao got his M.Sc degree in ecology from Fujian Normal University and joined the lab in Sep. 2022. His research is focused on the maintenance of rare species.



Jingyue Huang, PhD student
Jingyue got her B.Sc degree in ecology from Guangxi University and joined the lab in Sep. 2020. Her work is mainly about the abundance-center hypothesis.

Yongchao Liu, Master student
Yongchao got his B.Sc degree in forestry from Shandong Agricultural University and joined the lab in Sep. 2020. He is exploring the possibilities of using remote sensing data to estimate tree diversity in natural forests.

Yulin Zeng, Master student (co-supervised with Dr. Qingsong Yang)
Yulin got her B.Sc degree in natural resource management from Northeast China Forestry University and joined the lab in Sep. 2021. She is trying to understand the causes and consequences of structures of understory fern communities in the 20-ha forest dynamics plot in Tiantong National Forest Park.

You Li, Master student
You got his B.Sc degree in forestry from Anhui Agricultural University and joined the lab in Sep. 2021. He is widely interested in community ecology and biogeography.

Haoxiang Lu, Master student
Haoxiang got his B.Sc degree in Ecology from Yangzhou University and joined the lab in Sep. 2022. He is working on understanding the inter- and intraspecific variation in foliar spectral reflectances.
